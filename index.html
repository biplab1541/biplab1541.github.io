Portfolio / Projects

Road Accident Severity Prediction

In this project, I used backpropagation to train a neural network that predicts how severe an accident might be. The project also looks closely at road safety data to find important patterns and causes of accidents. This helps in reducing risks and gives useful information to policymakers for improving road safety. By combining prediction with detailed data analysis, the project supports better decisions to make roads safer. My main role was working on the front end to make the interface easy to use. On the back end, I focused on learning important concepts and applying them to build the needed features. Through this, I also gained a good understanding of machine learning.

Agora - Recommendation System

Agora uses a Collaborative Filtering algorithm, similar to what Netflix and Amazon use, to give users personalized recommendations. It looks at what users search for and finds patterns in their preferences and behavior. For example, Netflix suggests movies and shows based on what you've watched, and Amazon recommends products based on your past purchases and browsing. This algorithm studies user activity and finds similarities between users or items to suggest things that match a person’s interests. As a result, the app helps users easily discover new content, products, or services that they’re likely to enjoy—just like how Netflix and Amazon do.
